About Me

Coding Things

I love problem-solving and riddles. My journey to coding started many years ago via my passion for minesweeper, sudoku, and tetris. These games taught me how to think logically, to be patient when presented with a difficult obstacle, and eventually to want to build my own projects! I'm excited to begin my journey down this path.

Always Learning

After already completing a Masters degree in a different field, I worried that becoming a developer was out of my reach. Until one day I spoke to a software engineer who told me that I already had everything that I needed; a curious mind, and the willingness to put in the hours myself to build up the required knowledge. That night, I learned how to build my own blog website from scratch, and I've been addicted ever since!
